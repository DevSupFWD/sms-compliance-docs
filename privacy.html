FWDthink SMS Privacy Policy

SMS Text Message Service Privacy Statement

FWDthink respects your privacy. This SMS Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your mobile phone number and related information when you use our SMS authentication service.

Information We Collect

When you provide your mobile phone number for SMS authentication, we collect:

  • Your mobile phone number
  • Delivery confirmations and message status
  • Opt-in and opt-out requests
  • Authentication attempt timestamps

How We Use Your Information

Your mobile phone number and related SMS data is used exclusively for:

  • Sending one-time authentication codes (OTP)
  • Account verification and security notifications
  • Password reset verification
  • Two-factor authentication services
  • Responding to HELP and STOP requests

Message Types and Frequency

  • Message Types: Authentication codes, account verification codes, security notifications
  • Message Frequency: Message frequency varies based on your account activity and authentication needs
  • Typical Volume: 1-10 messages per month depending on usage

How We Protect Your Information

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your mobile phone number and SMS data, including:

  • Secure storage of phone numbers
  • Encrypted transmission of messages
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments
  • Compliance with applicable data protection laws

Information Sharing and Third Parties

Your mobile phone number and SMS opt-in information will NOT be:

  • Sold to third parties
  • Shared with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Used for any purpose other than authentication and account security
  • Disclosed to unauthorized parties

EXPLICIT STATEMENT: Mobile opt-in data and consent will NOT be shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share your information only when:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect our rights or property
  • Required for SMS service delivery (with our authorized SMS service providers who are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and are prohibited from using your data for any other purpose)

Your Rights and Choices

Opt-Out Rights:

  • Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe immediately
  • You will receive confirmation of your opt-out request
  • Opting out will disable SMS authentication for your account

Access and Control:

  • You can update your phone number in your account settings
  • You can re-enable SMS by replying START or updating your account preferences
  • You can contact us to request information about your SMS data

Support:

  • Reply HELP for assistance
  • Contact dev.support@fwdthink.com for questions about this policy

Consent and Agreement

By providing your mobile phone number and using our SMS authentication service, you consent to:

  • Receiving authentication-related text messages from FWDthink
  • Our collection and use of your phone number as described in this policy
  • Message and data rates may apply according to your carrier plan
  • The terms outlined in our SMS Terms & Conditions

Compliance with Regulations

This SMS Privacy Policy complies with:

  • TCPA (Telephone Consumer Protection Act) requirements
  • 10DLC (10-Digit Long Code) regulations
  • CTIA (Cellular Telecommunications Industry Association) guidelines
  • Applicable state and federal privacy laws

Data Retention

We retain your mobile phone number and SMS data:

  • For as long as your account remains active
  • As required by law or regulation
  • For legitimate business purposes (such as security and fraud prevention)
  • You may request deletion of your SMS data by contacting support

International Users

SMS services are currently available for US and Canadian phone numbers only. International data transfer and privacy laws may apply.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this SMS Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via:

  • Email notification to your registered email address
  • Notice in your account dashboard
  • Updated policy posting with revision date

Contact Information

For questions about this SMS Privacy Policy or our SMS services, email: dev.support@fwdthink.com.
