FWDthink SMS Terms & Conditions

SMS Text Messaging Service Terms

By providing your mobile phone number to FWDthink, you consent to receive SMS text messages from FWDthink for authentication and account security purposes.

Message Types and Purpose

You will receive the following types of messages from FWDthink:

  • One-time authentication codes (OTP)
  • Account verification codes
  • Password reset verification codes
  • Login security notifications

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your account activity. You may receive authentication messages when you:

  • Log into your FWDthink account
  • Reset your password
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Perform security-sensitive account actions

For recurring message programs: Message frequency varies from 1-10 messages per month depending on your usage patterns and authentication needs.

Costs and Charges

Message and data rates may apply according to your mobile carrier's plan. FWDthink does not charge for SMS messages, but your carrier may apply standard messaging rates.

Opt-In Consent

By providing your phone number during account setup or when enabling SMS authentication, you consent to receive text messages from FWDthink at the number provided. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. You can unsubscribe by replying STOP. Reply HELP for help. Mobile opt-in information will not be shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

View our Privacy Policy and these Terms & Conditions at the links provided in your account or campaign registration.

Support and Help

  • For help: Reply HELP to any message or contact us at dev.support@fwdthink.com
  • For support: Email dev.support@fwdthink.com or visit our support portal

How to Stop Messages (Opt-Out)

TO UNSUBSCRIBE: Reply STOP to any message from FWDthink to immediately stop receiving SMS messages. You will receive a confirmation message that you have been unsubscribed from our SMS service.

TO RESUME: Reply START to re-enable SMS authentication.

IMPORTANT: Opting out will disable SMS-based authentication for your account. You may need to use alternative authentication methods to access your account.

Privacy Policy: For more information about how we handle your data, please review our complete Privacy Policy at the link provided in your account settings.
